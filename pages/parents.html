The Importance of Speed

Why Speed?

Speed Kills

Reliable, explosive acceleration and blazing top end speeds will LITERALLY separate your child from the pack. These skills provide a major advantage that will get your child noticed on the court or field!

Transferable Skills

Though it may seem straightforward, sprinting is a series of extremely complex movements subjecting the body to high levels of force. As such, your child will develop high amounts of coordination, rhythm, strength, and power.
